Welcome to Grab Your Mat (Yoga studio with a difference)

Our goal is to help you reach an enhanced state of awareness and union with all, and that is where yoga practice in our studio comes into play. You become more aware of your mind, body and breath and learn to coordinate them. Meditation helps you to become more aware of your surroundings resulting in oneness with the universe.

Through the physical practice of asanas (poses) and pranayama (breathing practices) we become aware of the spiritual path of yoga. A clean body and mind is something that is of prime importance to reach more awakened states of consciousness and the eight limbs of yoga help us get there.

We aim to provide a safe, effective, customized and enjoyable yoga practice for people of all ages and levels of fitness. A range of practice options is available to ensure gradual development of strength, flexibility, balance and relaxation.

We also organize workshops, corporate classes, retreats designed to explore different aspects of yoga practice and how it can be used to shape and enhance our daily lives.

Who is Madhusatta Chaudhury?

Brief yoga journey

Practicing yoga since the tender age of 9 only to cure her congenital asthma, Madhusatta’s journey through yoga has been anything if not eventful.  After learning the ropes of therapeutic yoga, she was hooked. And once the asthma was under control, she chose not to walk away from the discipline. Instead, she graduated to participating in full-fledged competitive yoga. Madhusatta has won three district, three state and two national level titles in sub-junior and junior girls’ categories, in various competitions organized by the Federation of Yoga Training and Research Association of India.

Certifications

Practitioner of Ashtanga Vinyasa Yoga but is also proficient in Hatha and restorative yoga. She has completed her 250-hour teacher training certificate from Mysore under the instruction of Yogacharya Bharat Shetty in the tradition of Krishnamacharya (whose students included Pattabhi Jois and BKS Iyengar) and is a Yoga Alliance USA certified teacher. Certified Yoga Wellness instructor by the Ministry of Ayush (YCB level 2). Certified Yoga Teacher & Evaluator by the Ministry of Ayush (YCB level 3).

Educational Qualification

She has done her major in Physiology (Physiology Hons), thanks to which she has a very good understanding of the complexities of the human anatomy and physiology. Her MBA in Human Resource Development enables her to understand human psychology better and thereby help clients with counseling and stress management.

Corporate yoga session packages:

Grab Your Mat provides highly specialised corporate yoga sessions both online and offline. Our program includes a wide variety of sessions including meditation, desk yoga, and mat yoga depending on client requirement, time and space availability. Our corporate wellness programs are designed for small and large organisations, B schools and clubs. All sessions are designed for all levels of fitness and comfort level.

Why is yoga in office space important?

First and foremost our sessions are designed to create mental clarity and efficiency as well as alleviate several ailments that occur from prolonged sitting. Targets and performance goals at times make employees feel paranoid, demotivated and decrease productivity. A brief yoga session leaves them feeling refreshed and recharged and helps them being more creative and efficient at the work right after. Healthy mind and healthy body is equal to increased productivity. The cumulative effect of efficient workers boosts business as well as creates a healthy working environment for employees.
